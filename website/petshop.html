Welcome to VIAPets

Our name is an acronym for "Very Important Animal", as we believe every animal is important.

We sell animals of all ages. Currently we only have dogs, cats, birds, fish, various rodents and some reptiles.

We have a service, where we "recycle" pets, meaning if you, for any reason, cannot keep your pet, we can help you out.
